How to Make Biryani

  • Ingredients
  • Recipe
  • Tips

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 500g Chicken or Lamb
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 2 Onions, thinly sliced
  • 3 Tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 Green Chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Fresh Coriander Leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Mint Leaves, chopped
  • 4 Cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 inch Ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Saffron Strands, soaked in warm milk
  • 1/4 cup Ghee (clarified butter)
  • Salt to taste

Recipe

  1. Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat ghee in a large pan, fry onions until golden brown, remove half and set aside for garnish.
  3. Add ginger, garlic, green chilies, and tomatoes to the pan, cook until soft.
  4. Add chicken or lamb, yogurt, and spices. Cook until the meat is tender.
  5. Boil rice until half-cooked, drain and layer over the meat mixture.
  6. Sprinkle coriander, mint, and saffron milk over the rice.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat until rice is fully cooked and flavors are infused.
  8. Garnish with fried onions and serve hot.

Tips

  • Use good quality Basmati rice for the best results.
  • Marinate the meat for at least 2 hours for enhanced flavor.
  • You can add fried cashews and raisins for extra richness.
  • Serve with raita (yogurt sauce) and salad for a complete meal.
